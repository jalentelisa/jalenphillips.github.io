Faithful Tithers

Bio for project:

Koinonia Christian Center is a religious institution with a growing congregation. They are fans of digital giving, and have made accommodations for those that may have CashApp, PayPal, etc. Third-party processors like these are not always reliable. Because of the various methods of giving, the administrative board has trouble keeping track of incoming finances.
Goal for Project: The goal is to design an app that helps minimize multiple payment options and synchronize tithing.
My Role: Design Consultant

Data Collecting

A form was sent out to current members, including the administrative board, to assess the expectations for the app. Once all the forms were submitted, a process of data screening was done to identify key goals and expectations that the church has.

Goals:

  • Tithe tracking
  • Detailed reports
  • Multiple giving options
Frustrations/Pain Points:
  • Complicated setup for visitors
  • Security and Data breaches

Based on the data, I prepared user personas to give examples of who is using the app.

Sketches and Wireframes

My role as the design consultant was to create a design for the app based on the data gathered. These are examples of wireframes and low-fidelity prototypes that gave visual context of the primary functions of the app. The design focused on the primary goals the users desired.
