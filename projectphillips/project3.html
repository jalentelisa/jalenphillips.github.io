BookWorms

Bio for assignment:

Create a Logo

The assignment is to create a logo, brand identify, and some form of marketing material for the company. Be creative and have fun!

For my assignment, I created a fictional bookstore by the name of BookWorms. Bookworm is a recognized term for people who either enjoy reading, or any form of academics. They are also passionate about literature; they typically thrive in environments that are peaceful and personable. The concept of the bookstore logo is to be both playful and easy going. This store will be a place where customers come for coffee and a quick chat. They can also partake in reading clubs, and or game nights.
