Landing Page Redesign Concept and Event Flyer

KLAP Inc is a non-profit apprenticeship program that primarily focuses on skills needed in the low voltage industry. They needed to boost their engagement on social media platforms and also promote the new C.O.R.E Program. I volunteered as a design consultant to redesign their webpage and create promotional flyers for social media.

The design and concept example in slide form:

Before and after mockup:

We retained the core elements of the landing page, focusing on displaying the programs, testimonials, and application options. The color scheme was chosen by vote. While the electric green was ideal, it clashed with the blue. To resolve this, we opted for a darker blue and reduced the saturation of the electric green. Our overall goal was to ensure clear text visibility and to guide the viewer's eye toward different focal points as they scroll. The bold design of the final section is an excellent spot to include a form.
